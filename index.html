About

I’m a PhD student in the MESL Lab at UC San Diego advised by Prof. Rajesh Gupta. My work focuses on algorithm–architecture co‑design for time‑series inference on edge devices—especially Human Activity Recognition and fall‑detection—spanning model design, accelerator RTL, and systems.

Keywords: Time‑series ML, LNN/CNN‑LNN, ConvLSTM, early‑exit, hardware accelerators
